Your privacy is our priority. We only share your data when it is absolutely necessary, and always with your awareness or consent.   • Contractual Necessity: If we have a contract with you, we process your personal information as needed to fulfill that contract.
  • Legal Requirements: We may be required to disclose certain information to comply with legal obligations such as court orders, subpoenas, or official government requests. In such cases, we will only provide the minimum information legally required — and never more.

Specifically, we may share your data in the following situations:

  • Trusted Third-Party Services: We work with selected vendors to support services such as hosting, analytics, communication, and infrastructure. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. You are always in control, and we commit to transparency in every part of our data handling process.

3. Data Security and Retention

We take data security seriously. All personal information is protected using industry-standard technical and organizational measures. We only work with trusted services and infrastructure providers that follow strict security practices. Every effort is made to keep your data safe from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss.

However, while we strive for maximum security, no method of digital transmission or storage is completely risk-free. We cannot fully guarantee that malicious actors (e.g., hackers or cybercriminals) will never breach our systems, despite all precautions. In the unlikely event of a security incident, we will act swiftly and transparently.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to deliver our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Once your data is no longer needed — or when your account is deleted — we will securely delete or anonymize it. In rare cases where deletion is temporarily impossible (e.g., data stored in encrypted backups), we will restrict further access and processing until secure deletion is feasible.

We never keep your data "just in case." If we have no legal or business reason to retain it, we remove it — cleanly and safely.
